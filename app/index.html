Early School Leaving (ESL). Dataset metadata.

The number of early school leaving in secondary education and college. Early school leavers are young people that leave their educational system before the age of 23 without acquiring a startersqualification. A startersqualification is a diploma at a minimum finished high school level. The amount of early school leavers is shown per educational system and various other background characteristics.
